Evolution of a phrase

A simple simulation of evolution by cumulative selection. Type in a sentence, and the computer algorithm will generate random phrases. The letters that 'survive' in the randomly generated phrase are the ones which resemble the target. You will notice that after several 'generations' of sentences, the generated phrases start to resemble the target more and more. "Random variation, nonrandom selection". This evolution algorithm was inspired by professor Richard Dawkins in his book The blind Watch Maker
